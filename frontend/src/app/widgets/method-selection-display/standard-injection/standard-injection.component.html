Standard Injection Instructions


Before the first sample of the day AND after the last sample of the day, run a QC.


    play_circle_outline
  1. Click the "Standard Injection" button in the "Quick Access" panel. You will need to wait [6] minutues prior ro injecting the sample. This time will vary depending on the method you select.
    2. play_circle_outline
  2. Inject the sample when the pop-up message appears. This will sample for CT A. Indicate that a liquid sample was injected into CT A using the checkbox below.
    3. play_circle_outline
  3. Wait [6] minutes for CT B to switch from "Analyzing" to "Sampling".
    4. play_circle_outline
  4. Inject the sample when the pop-up message appears. This will sample for CT B. Indicate that a liquid sample was injected into CT B using the checkbox below.
    5. play_circle_outline
  5. Verify that the injection amount is accurate or update the injection amount as needed. Click the "Standard Injections Complete" button below.
